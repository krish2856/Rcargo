Demonstration & Walkthrough

New agents can quickly familiarize themselves with our automated booking engine. Ensure that printer defaults are set to standard sizes for thermal label receipts.

  • Select the Branch Office login, enter your city branch (e.g. ahmedabad or mumbai), username (ahmedabad_branch), and password (password123).
  • Enter cargo weight, packaging type, and consignment values. The system automatically computes the basic tariffs, surcharges, and 18% GST.
  • Choose payment terms (Paid / To Pay / Account) and click Save & Print to produce a thermal docket layout.
